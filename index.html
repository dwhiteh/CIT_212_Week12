United States Navy Surface Fleet

Since the United States Navy's birthday on October 13, 1775, the surface fleet has been the backbone of the strongest Navy in the world even as the Navy has expanded into air and subsurface operations. The US Navy employs a variety of ships including amphibious assault, oil tankers, mine countermeasure, destroyers, cruisers, and aircraft carriers.
